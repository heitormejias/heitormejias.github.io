Residential Solution

The use of our solution for residential use can be through an analysis of the need and location to the design of necessity. Can be off grid and grid tie as specifying its distributor. Furthermore, the system can include a dedicated heating generator (water and the environment) and a cooling system. The complete system installed can supply all the energy needed in the home, or just what it was designed. If necessary, generator may be added to increase the energy supplied. Additionally it is possible to develop a solution for more than one residence. For example, a residential complex could share a scaled system to meet individual and common demand. Please, let us know your need.

Industry

As we know, large industries are large users of energy. This means you need larger or multiple generators. But that would be no problem. Our solution is scalable. We could evaluate the best benefit and lower cost. For example, a dedicated cooling system could provide a good cost savings to the industry. Our team is on hand to evaluate the best solution with the best benefit for your industry.

Street lighting

An interesting application of our solution would be in the public lighting. With a system dimensioned just to light a lamp in lampposts, we could light up a whole city or even a neighborhood. During the day, when there is no need for light, energy could be used to irrigate public gardens, for example.

Meteorology

Who's meteorology area knows that it is essential to have climate monitoring points in the most points possible to increase the assertiveness of forecasts. How our solution can be installed anywhere without relying on the availability of power or batteries and generators, we could build in remote areas, climate monitoring systems that transmit information via satellite or mobile networks.

Air traffic control

Similarly the climatic monitoring functions, the need for air traffic control points might help in the navigation of aircraft. Imagine an air traffic control point installed in the middle of the Amazon rainforest?. Our solution helps make this a reality. Despite knowing that maintenance in remote areas can be a problem, we would have to evaluate the benefit that this solution could bring to air navigation. As the evaluation, we could have a system with a high level of redundancy, making the availability as high as possible.

Telecommunications

We know that today the dependence of communication is extremely important in a globalized world. Internet, social networks, private networks, make the world increasingly connected. Mobile communications companies today already outnumber fixed. However, the installation of radio stations to increase the coverage of cellular networks depends on electricity. Our solution allows radio stations to be installed which best suits operators. Remote or not. The same solution is also convenient for WIFI services, or monitoring services through cameras of surveillance. It seems we can help make our world more connected. Contact us.
